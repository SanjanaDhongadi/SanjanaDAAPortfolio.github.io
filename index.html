ABOUT COURSE

1.WHAT IS THE COURSE ABOUT?




-->The Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) course is about learning how to create efficient algorithms to solve complex problems. It focuses on understanding algorithm design techniques(like divide and conquer, greedy, and dynamic programming) and analyzing their performance in terms of time and space complexity.
This course emphasizes:
- The systematic design of algorithms.
- The analysis of their time and space complexities.
- The application of mathematical tools to compare algorithm performance.
By studying this, I have gained the skills to approach problems systematically, design solutions, and measure their efficiency.
